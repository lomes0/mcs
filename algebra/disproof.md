------

If $H,K < G$  cyclic,  $|K|=|H|$. 

Then either $ K\cap H = \empty$ or $K=H$.



##### Counter Example

Let $Q_8$ be the quaternion group. Then we have:
$$
[i] \cap [j] = \{-1,1\}
$$
While $[i]\neq[j]$, and $4=|[i]|=|[j]|$.



------



If any $H < G$ is cyclic, then G is cyclic.
